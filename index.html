What is this site?

Webcompat.com is a community of volunteers supported by Mozilla that believe in an open and compatible web. The goal is to ensure that internet users everywhere have a choice of which web browser or device they would like to access the web on. We do this through finding compatibility issues on websites, understanding the issue, contacting the website and helping to resolve. The internet is huge and we are always looking for people to help, learn how you can get involved.

Users have reported a problem with your website not working properly for Mozilla Firefox Browsers. You may be able to help. If you know someone, potentially in the Communications, Marketing or Technology departments, please pass along this information.
